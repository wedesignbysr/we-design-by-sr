Project Overview

Spark Interactive aimed to create a concept website that pushed creative boundaries while showcasing technical expertise. The goal was to demonstrate experimental layouts, bold visuals, and engaging interactions that stand out in a competitive digital space.

Solution & Approach

We designed an experimental interface combining micro-interactions, hover animations, and scroll-driven motion. High-contrast colours and bold typography were used to highlight innovation while maintaining usability and responsiveness across devices.

Key Features

Micro-Interactions

Hover effects and motion feedback enhance user engagement.

Dynamic elements respond as users explore the site.

Experimental Layouts

Creative, non-traditional structures designed to inspire.

Responsive Design

Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile without losing impact.
